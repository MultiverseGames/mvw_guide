Bank

A safe place to store your income from attacks; however, there is a 2% banking fee to deposit currency into the bank.Items in your bank, can not be stolen, nor can it be used for upkeep of your ships.

Bank Upgrades

Metals and Foods can be upgraded for their resources. You can upgrade as much as you like from here.

Metal Storage - Storage capacity is now based on upgrades. Formula: (1.6^ upgrade level)*12000

Food Storage - Storage capacity also based on upgrades. Formula (1.6^ upgrade level)*200000
