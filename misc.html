Our Logo

The Multiverse Warriors Logo comprises of several planets occupying the same space, slightly overlapping. We welcome players to spread the word about it.

Forums / Chat Rooms

We provide alternative methods to communicate with other players via our Forums and Chat rooms. The Forum and Chat rooms are connected to each other and are separate to the game. Feel free to create your account and persona on the forums. We encourage all players to participate and create a community for the game. As we grow, we will require additional help from players to moderate forums / chat rooms. We may choose to reimburse players for their time, depending on how much is given / dedicated to ensuring the community is well maintained.

As in the game, language, abuse, inciting hatred or dissent towards any Governments, Religious Groups, peoples, or Race will NOT be tolerated, and you will no longer be welcome to play our game.

Registration Issues

I Registered but got no email, is something we commonly hear. In most scenarios, our emails may have ended up in your SPAM / Junkmail folder, we're working to try and rectify this, but if you check there, and mark it as Not Spam, you can help us help you. If you didn't receive an email, contact us, and we will help you as best we can!
